[   OUR RESTAURANT   ]

The Orient Restaurant is a local family restaurant that first opened in 1978. Currently run by the second generation, we have established ourselves as a quaint and reputable spot to enjoy the best of traditional Asian cuisine in an open casual setting.

Drop by, say hi, and taste what we have to offer. I'd say we are all pretty amiable folks here.

[   DINNER BUFFET   ]

Our dinner buffet runs every night from 5-8pm, which features over 25 hot entrées, soup, a salad bar, and a variety of bite-sized desserts.

Try out our unique "buffet-to-go" option where you can fill up a container with as much food as you can cram in beneath the lid*, all with a side of soup / salad / dessert.

* { "beneath the lid" may be a bit of a relative term here... }

[   TAKEOUT & DELIVERY   ]

Phone in your order ahead of time and we'll get your order ready in a jiffy. We also offer a 10% discount on all pick-up orders $25 and above.

Get your meal delivered right to your door with our evening delivery service which starts at 4pm every night.
