DIM SUM

Originating in traditional teahouses, the DIM SUM cuisine offers a variety of steamed dumplings, buns, and other miniature dishes.
Served between 11:00am to 2:00pm daily, come and try our selection of made-to-order dim sum which pairs delightfully with our house jasmine-infused green tea.
